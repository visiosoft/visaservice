Singapore Work Permit Guide 2024

Understanding Work Permits in Singapore

The Work Permit is designed for semi-skilled foreign workers in specific sectors including construction, manufacturing, marine shipyard, process, and services sectors. Different rules apply for foreign domestic workers.

Sector-Specific Requirements

Construction Sector:
  • Basic Skilled (R2) or Higher Skilled (R1) certification
  • Pass Construction Safety Orientation Course
  • Maximum age: 60 years old
  • From approved source countries
Manufacturing Sector:
  • Relevant skills certification
  • Maximum age: 50 years old
  • From approved source countries
  • Pass medical examination
Marine Sector:
  • Marine Safety Course certification
  • Maximum age: 50 years old
  • From approved source countries
  • Industry-specific skills certification
Domestic Workers:
  • Age: 23-50 years old
  • 8 years of formal education
  • Pass Entry Test
  • Complete Settling-In Programme

Application Process

  1. Initial Application

    Employer submits application via WP Online

  2. Security Bond

    Purchase $5,000 security bond

  3. Medical Examination

    Worker completes medical screening

  4. Thumb Printing

    Worker registers fingerprints

  5. Safety Courses

    Complete required safety training

  6. Work Permit Card

    Collect physical card

Required Documents

Basic Documents:
  • Valid passport (at least 7 months validity)
  • Completed application form
  • Recent passport-size photo
  • Educational certificates
  • Skills certification
  • Medical examination report
Employer Documents:
  • Company's registration details
  • Quota eligibility proof
  • Security bond
  • Insurance policies

Employer Responsibilities

  • Provide acceptable accommodation
  • Ensure medical insurance coverage
  • Pay monthly levy
  • Maintain safety standards
  • Timely salary payment
  • Work injury compensation insurance
  • Repatriation responsibility

Common Issues & Solutions

  • Quota exceeded - Check MOM calculator
  • Incomplete documentation - Use checklist
  • Failed medical - Pre-screening recommended
  • Source country restrictions - Check eligibility
  • Skills certification issues - Verify requirements

Renewal Process

Renewal Requirements:
  • Valid passport
  • Medical examination
  • Updated skills certification
  • Quota availability
  • Good employment record

Apply for renewal 6 weeks before expiry

Quick Facts
  • Processing: 1-3 weeks
  • Validity: Up to 2 years
  • Security Bond: $5,000
  • Quota: Sector-based
  • Insurance: Mandatory
