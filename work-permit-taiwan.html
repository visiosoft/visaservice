Taiwan Work Permit Guide 2024

Latest Update (March 2024): Taiwan has updated its work permit requirements and application procedures. This guide reflects the latest changes.

Overview of Taiwan Work Permit

A Taiwan Work Permit is a legal document that allows foreign nationals to work in Taiwan. Unlike the Employment Gold Card, this permit is tied to a specific employer and requires company sponsorship.

Key Features
  • Valid for 1-3 years
  • Employer-specific permit
  • Renewable based on employment contract
  • Allows legal employment in Taiwan
  • Basis for ARC application

Eligibility Requirements

Basic Requirements
  • Valid job offer from a Taiwan-based employer
  • Relevant qualifications and experience
  • Minimum salary requirement (varies by industry)
  • Clean criminal record
  • Valid passport with at least 6 months validity
Professional Requirements
  • Bachelor's degree or higher in relevant field
  • At least 2 years of work experience
  • Professional certifications (if applicable)
  • Language proficiency (varies by position)

Required Documents

Personal Documents
  • Passport copy (valid for at least 6 months)
  • Recent passport-sized photos
  • Resume/CV
  • Educational certificates
  • Work experience letters
  • Criminal record check
Employment Documents
  • Employment contract
  • Job description
  • Company registration documents
  • Employer's tax registration
  • Salary statements
All documents must be:
  • Translated into Chinese
  • Notarized and authenticated
  • Less than 6 months old

Application Process

  1. Employer Application

    Your Taiwan employer must first apply for permission to hire foreign workers.

  2. Document Preparation

    Gather and authenticate all required documents.

  3. Work Permit Application

    Submit application to the Workforce Development Agency.

  4. Visa Application

    Apply for a resident visa at Taiwan's overseas mission.

  5. ARC Application

    Apply for an Alien Resident Certificate within 15 days of arrival.

Processing Time and Fees

Item Processing Time Fee (TWD)
Work Permit 7-10 working days 1,000
Resident Visa 5-7 working days 2,200
ARC 10-14 working days 1,000
