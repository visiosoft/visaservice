Taiwan Global Entry Permit (GEP) Guide 2024

Latest Update (March 2024): Taiwan has expanded the GEP program with new benefits and streamlined application process for highly skilled professionals.

What is the Taiwan GEP?

The Taiwan Global Entry Permit (GEP) is a special visa program designed for highly skilled foreign professionals. It combines work permit, resident visa, Alien Resident Certificate (ARC), and re-entry permit into a single card, offering a streamlined path for talented individuals to work and live in Taiwan.

Key Benefits

  • 4-in-1 card (work permit, resident visa, ARC, re-entry permit)
  • Valid for up to 3 years
  • Job flexibility (no employer sponsorship required)
  • Family can join with dependent visas
  • Tax benefits for first 3 years
  • Access to Taiwan's healthcare system

Eligible Professional Fields

Eight Major Professional Areas:
  • Science & Technology
  • Economics & Finance
  • Education & Teaching
  • Culture & Arts
  • Sports & Recreation
  • Law & Legal Services
  • Architectural Design
  • Health & Medicine

Eligibility Requirements

Qualification Criteria:
  • Outstanding professional achievements
  • Minimum 5 years work experience in relevant field
  • Clean criminal record
  • Meet salary threshold or special expertise criteria
  • Valid passport with at least 6 months validity
Applicants must meet at least one qualification criteria in their professional field.

Application Process

  1. Online Application

    Submit application through the Taiwan GEP website

  2. Document Verification

    Upload required documents and credentials

  3. Review Process

    Application review by relevant government agencies

  4. Card Collection

    Collect GEP card in Taiwan or at overseas mission

Required Documents

  • Completed online application form
  • Professional certificates and diplomas
  • Work experience proof
  • Passport copy
  • Professional achievement evidence
  • Police clearance certificate
  • Passport-size photos
  • CV/Resume
  • Income proof or financial statements

Fees and Processing Times

Fee Type Cost (NT$) Processing Time
Application Fee 3,700 30 working days
Card Issuance 1,000 5-7 working days
Express Processing 5,000 15 working days

Tax Benefits

Special Tax Incentives:
  • 50% tax exemption on salary income exceeding NT$3 million
  • Tax benefits valid for first 3 years
  • Simplified tax filing process
  • Foreign-sourced income exemption

Renewal and Permanent Residency

Options After Initial Period:
  • Renewable for additional 3-year periods
  • Eligible for APRC after 5 years
  • Continuous employment not required
  • Flexible work arrangements allowed
