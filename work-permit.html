Understanding Work Permits

Work Permits are for foreign workers in manual labor positions across various sectors including construction, manufacturing, marine, process, and services sectors. Different rules apply for domestic workers.

Sector Categories

  • Construction Sector
  • Manufacturing Sector
  • Marine Sector
  • Process Sector
  • Services Sector
  • Domestic Work Sector

Basic Requirements

General Requirements:
  • Age: 18-50 years old (varies by sector)
  • From approved source countries
  • Pass medical examination
  • Valid passport
  • Employer's eligibility
Sector-Specific Requirements:
  • Construction: BCAA certification
  • Manufacturing: Relevant experience
  • Marine: Safety certification
  • Domestic: Entry test & training

Quota and Levy System

Dependency Ceiling (2024):
  • Construction: 1 local : 7 foreign
  • Manufacturing: 1 local : 5 foreign
  • Services: 1 local : 3 foreign
  • Marine: 1 local : 5 foreign
Monthly Levy Rates:
Sector Basic Tier Higher Tier
Construction $300 $600
Manufacturing $250 $550
Marine $300 $600

Required Documents

Essential Documents:
  • Completed application form
  • Passport copy
  • Educational certificates
  • Skills certification
  • Medical examination report
  • Security bond
  • Insurance coverage

Application Process

  1. Employer Application

    Submit application via WP Online

  2. Security Bond

    Purchase $5,000 security bond

  3. Medical Examination

    Complete medical screening

  4. Approval & IPA

    Receive In-Principle Approval

  5. Work Permit Card

    Collect after arrival

Employer Responsibilities

  • Provide proper accommodation
  • Medical insurance coverage
  • Safety training and equipment
  • Timely salary payment
  • Work injury compensation
  • Maintain quota compliance
  • Repatriation responsibility

Common Issues & Solutions

  • Quota exceeded
  • Incomplete documentation
  • Medical issues
  • Source country restrictions
  • Skills certification issues

Renewal Process

Renewal Requirements:
  • Valid passport
  • Medical examination
  • Updated skills certification
  • Quota availability
  • Good employment record

Apply for renewal 3 months before expiry

Quick Facts
  • Processing: 1-3 weeks
  • Validity: Up to 2 years
  • Security Bond: $5,000
  • Quota: Sector-based
  • Insurance: Mandatory
