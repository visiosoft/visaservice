Taiwan Quality Migrant Admission Scheme (QMAS) Guide 2024

Latest Update (March 2024): Taiwan has updated the QMAS points system and introduced new categories for emerging industries.

What is the Taiwan QMAS?

The Taiwan Quality Migrant Admission Scheme (QMAS) is a points-based immigration program designed to attract highly skilled professionals to Taiwan. This scheme allows qualified individuals to enter and settle in Taiwan without securing a job offer first.

Key Benefits

  • No job offer required before application
  • Initial stay period of 2 years
  • Dependents can join
  • Path to permanent residency
  • Access to healthcare and social benefits
  • Work flexibility across employers

Points System

Assessment Categories:
Category Maximum Points
Age 30
Academic/Professional Qualifications 70
Work Experience 55
Language Skills 20
Family Background 20
Achievement Awards 15
Minimum passing score: 160 points out of 210

Eligibility Requirements

Basic Requirements:
  • Age 18-55
  • Good education background (Bachelor's degree minimum)
  • Clean criminal record
  • Financial self-sufficiency
  • Proficiency in Mandarin or English
  • Valid passport with at least 6 months validity

Application Process

  1. Online Pre-assessment

    Complete the points calculator to assess eligibility

  2. Document Preparation

    Gather and authenticate required documents

  3. Application Submission

    Submit application to Taiwan overseas mission

  4. Assessment

    Application review by immigration authorities

  5. Approval and Entry

    Receive entry permit and enter Taiwan

Required Documents

  • Completed application form
  • Recent passport photos
  • Valid passport copy
  • Educational certificates
  • Professional qualifications
  • Work experience letters
  • Language proficiency certificates
  • Police clearance certificate
  • Bank statements (last 3 months)
  • Achievement certificates (if any)

Fees and Processing Times

Fee Type Cost (NT$) Processing Time
Application Fee 4,000 4-6 weeks
Entry Permit 2,000 1-2 weeks
ARC Issuance 1,000 2-3 weeks

Extension and Settlement

Long-term Options:
  • Initial period: 2 years
  • Extension: 3 years upon approval
  • APRC eligibility: After 5 years of residence
  • Citizenship option: After 7 years
