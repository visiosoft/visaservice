Current H1B Processing Times (2024)

As of 2024, the processing times for H1B visas vary depending on the service center and the type of processing requested. Here's a breakdown of current processing times:

  • Premium Processing: 15 calendar days
  • Regular Processing: 2-4 months
  • Extension Processing: 2-3 months

Factors Affecting Processing Time

Several factors can influence your H1B visa processing time:

  • Service Center workload
  • Type of processing requested
  • Completeness of application
  • Request for Evidence (RFE) responses

How to Check Your Case Status

You can check your H1B case status online using your receipt number through the USCIS Case Status Online tool.

Tips to Expedite Processing

  • Choose premium processing if available
  • Ensure all documents are properly prepared
  • Respond to RFEs promptly
  • Work with an experienced immigration attorney
