Step-by-Step Guide for Pakistani Citizens

Applying for a Schengen visa from Pakistan requires careful preparation and attention to detail. Here's your comprehensive guide:

1. Determine Your Visa Type

  • Tourist Visa (Most Common)
  • Business Visa
  • Family Visit Visa
  • Student Visa

2. Required Documents

  • Valid Pakistani Passport
  • Visa Application Form
  • Recent Photographs
  • Travel Insurance
  • Bank Statements
  • Flight Itinerary
  • Hotel Bookings
  • Employment Letter

3. Application Process

  1. Choose the correct embassy/consulate
  2. Schedule an appointment
  3. Prepare all required documents
  4. Submit your application
  5. Pay the visa fee
  6. Attend biometric appointment

4. Processing Time and Fees

Visa Type Processing Time Fee (EUR)
Tourist Visa 15-30 days 80
Business Visa 15-30 days 80
Student Visa 30-60 days 80

5. Tips for Successful Application

  • Apply well in advance (3-6 months before travel)
  • Ensure all documents are properly attested
  • Maintain sufficient bank balance
  • Provide clear travel itinerary
  • Be honest in your application

6. Common Reasons for Rejection

  • Insufficient financial means
  • Incomplete documentation
  • Unclear travel purpose
  • Previous visa violations
  • Security concerns
