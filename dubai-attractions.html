Dubai Tourist Attractions Guide 2024

Quick Overview

Discover Dubai's most iconic attractions and experiences. This comprehensive guide covers must-visit places, activities, and tips for an unforgettable Dubai experience.

Iconic Landmarks

Burj Khalifa

The world's tallest building offers breathtaking views from its observation decks. Experience the city from new heights at the 124th and 148th floors.

  • Opening hours: 9:00 AM - 11:00 PM
  • Entry fee: AED 135-370
  • Best time to visit: Sunset

Burj Al Arab

The iconic sail-shaped hotel is a symbol of Dubai's luxury and innovation. While staying is expensive, you can visit for afternoon tea or dinner.

  • Location: Jumeirah Beach
  • Afternoon tea: AED 490
  • Dinner: AED 800+

Dubai Mall

The world's largest shopping mall features over 1,200 shops, an aquarium, and the famous Dubai Fountain show.

  • Opening hours: 10:00 AM - 12:00 AM
  • Fountain show: Every 30 minutes
  • Free entry

Desert Experiences

Desert Safari

Experience the thrill of dune bashing, camel riding, and traditional entertainment under the stars.

  • Duration: 6-7 hours
  • Price: AED 150-300
  • Includes: Dinner and entertainment

Hot Air Balloon

Float above the desert at sunrise for a magical experience with stunning views.

  • Duration: 1 hour
  • Price: AED 1,200-1,500
  • Includes: Breakfast

Cultural Experiences

Jumeirah Mosque

One of Dubai's most beautiful mosques, offering guided tours for non-Muslims.

  • Tour times: 10:00 AM, Saturday-Thursday
  • Entry fee: AED 35
  • Includes: Traditional refreshments

Dubai Creek & Souks

Experience traditional Dubai with abra rides and shopping in the historic souks.

  • Abra ride: AED 1
  • Best time: Evening
  • Location: Deira

Adventure Activities

Ski Dubai

Indoor skiing and snowboarding in the Mall of the Emirates.

  • Opening hours: 10:00 AM - 11:00 PM
  • Entry fee: AED 190-250
  • Includes: Equipment rental

Aquaventure Waterpark

Thrilling water slides and marine life experiences at Atlantis The Royal.

  • Opening hours: 10:00 AM - 6:00 PM
  • Entry fee: AED 299
  • Includes: All attractions

Travel Tips

Best Time to Visit

  • November to March: Pleasant weather
  • April to October: Hot but good hotel rates
  • Ramadan: Special cultural experience

Money-Saving Tips

  • Book attractions online
  • Use public transport
  • Visit during off-peak hours
  • Look for combo tickets
