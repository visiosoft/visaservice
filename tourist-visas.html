Dubai Tourist Visa for Pakistanis - Complete Guide 2024

Key Points

  • Multiple visa types available (30-day, 60-day, 90-day)
  • Processing time: 3-5 working days
  • Valid for 60 days from issue date
  • Multiple entry options available
  • No interview required

Types of Dubai Tourist Visas

Visa Type Duration Entry Type Processing Time Price Range
30-Day Tourist Visa 30 days Single/Multiple 3-5 days PKR 25,000-35,000
60-Day Tourist Visa 60 days Single/Multiple 3-5 days PKR 35,000-45,000
90-Day Tourist Visa 90 days Single/Multiple 3-5 days PKR 45,000-55,000

Required Documents

Basic Requirements

  • Valid Pakistani passport (6 months validity)
  • Passport-size photographs (white background)
  • Bank statements (last 6 months)
  • Employment letter or business proof
  • Travel insurance
  • Hotel booking confirmation
  • Return flight tickets

Additional Requirements

  • Marriage certificate (if married)
  • Children's birth certificates (if traveling with children)
  • No Objection Certificate (NOC) from employer
  • Property documents (if self-employed)

Application Process

  1. Choose your visa type and duration
  2. Gather all required documents
  3. Submit application through authorized agent
  4. Pay visa fees
  5. Wait for visa processing
  6. Receive e-visa via email
  7. Print visa and carry with passport

Important Information

Visa Validity

  • Visa is valid for 60 days from issue date
  • Must enter Dubai within 60 days of visa issue
  • Stay duration depends on visa type (30/60/90 days)

Travel Tips

  • Book flights after visa approval
  • Ensure hotel booking matches visa duration
  • Carry sufficient funds for stay
  • Get comprehensive travel insurance
