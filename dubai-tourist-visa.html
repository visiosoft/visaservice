Dubai Tourist Visa Guide 2024

Quick Overview

Dubai offers various tourist visa options for international visitors. This comprehensive guide covers everything you need to know about Dubai tourist visas in 2024, from types and requirements to the application process and expert tips. For general information about tourist visas worldwide, visit our Complete Guide to Tourist Visas.

Types of Dubai Tourist Visas

30-Day Tourist Visa

The most common type of tourist visa for Dubai. Valid for 30 days from entry.

  • Single entry
  • Extendable for 30 days
  • Valid for tourism only
  • No work allowed

90-Day Tourist Visa

Longer stay option for extended tourism visits.

  • Single entry
  • Valid for 90 days
  • Tourism purposes only
  • No work permitted

Multiple Entry Tourist Visa

Ideal for frequent visitors to Dubai.

  • Multiple entries
  • Valid for 6 months
  • 30 days per stay
  • Tourism only

Basic Requirements

Valid passport with at least 6 months validity
Passport-sized photographs (white background)
Travel insurance (mandatory)
Bank statements (last 3 months)
Hotel booking confirmation
Return flight tickets

Application Process

Step 1: Document Preparation

Gather all required documents and ensure they meet specifications

Step 2: Choose Application Method

Select between online application or through a travel agency

Step 3: Submit Application

Complete application form and submit with required documents

Step 4: Payment

Pay visa fees and processing charges

Step 5: Processing

Wait for visa processing and approval

Step 6: Visa Collection

Receive approved visa electronically or through courier
