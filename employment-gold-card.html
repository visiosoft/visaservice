Taiwan Employment Gold Card Guide

Your comprehensive guide to obtaining Taiwan's prestigious Employment Gold Card - a 4-in-1 permit for work, residence, visa, and re-entry

What is the Employment Gold Card?

The Taiwan Employment Gold Card is a special visa launched under the Act for the Recruitment and Employment of Foreign Professionals. It combines work permit, residence visa, Alien Resident Certificate (ARC), and multiple re-entry permit into a single card, valid for 1-3 years.

Eligibility Requirements

  • Proven expertise in eligible professional fields
  • Minimum monthly salary of NT$160,000 in previous job
  • Relevant work experience or outstanding achievements
  • Clean criminal record
  • Professional certifications (if applicable)

Gold Card Benefits

Employment Benefits
  • Freedom to work or start a business
  • No employer sponsorship needed
  • Multiple job flexibility
  • Entrepreneurship opportunities
Tax Benefits
  • 50% tax exemption on income above NT$3 million
  • Tax consultation services
  • Dependent tax benefits
Family Benefits
  • Spouse and children visas
  • Healthcare coverage
  • Education access

Application Process

Step 1: Online Application

Submit application through the Taiwan Gold Card website with required documents.

Step 2: Qualification Assessment

Relevant ministry reviews professional qualifications.

Step 3: Immigration Review

National Immigration Agency reviews application.

Step 4: Card Collection

Collect Gold Card in person in Taiwan or at overseas representative office.

Required Documents

  • Passport (valid for at least 6 months)
  • Professional certificates/diplomas
  • Proof of work experience
  • Salary proof or tax returns
  • Criminal record check
  • Passport photo

Quick Facts

  • Validity: 1-3 years
  • Processing Time: 30-60 days
  • Application Fee: NT$3,100
  • Renewal: Possible
  • Work Permit: Included
