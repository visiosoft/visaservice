Taiwan Investment Visa Guide 2024

Latest Update (March 2024): Taiwan has updated its investment visa requirements and introduced new incentives for foreign investors.

What is the Taiwan Investment Visa?

The Taiwan Investment Visa (Investor Visa) is designed for foreign nationals who wish to invest in and establish businesses in Taiwan. This visa category allows investors to live and conduct business in Taiwan while contributing to the country's economic growth.

Key Benefits

  • Long-term residency in Taiwan
  • Ability to bring family members
  • Access to Taiwan's healthcare system
  • Business ownership rights
  • Path to permanent residency (APRC)

Investment Requirements

Minimum Investment Options:
  • NT$6 million (approximately US$200,000) in a new business
  • NT$15 million in government bonds or time deposits
  • Investment in approved sectors (technology, manufacturing, services)
Investment amounts must be maintained throughout the visa period.

Eligibility Requirements

Primary Requirements:
  • Meet minimum investment threshold
  • Clean criminal record
  • Valid passport with at least 6 months validity
  • Proof of financial capability
  • Detailed business plan (for new businesses)
  • Health examination certificate

Application Process

  1. Business Registration

    Register your company with the Ministry of Economic Affairs

  2. Investment Verification

    Obtain investment approval from relevant authorities

  3. Visa Application

    Submit application to Taiwan overseas mission

  4. ARC Application

    Apply for Alien Resident Certificate after arrival

Required Documents

  • Completed visa application form
  • Passport (original and copy)
  • Investment verification documents
  • Business registration certificates
  • Financial statements
  • Business plan
  • Health examination certificate
  • Police clearance certificate
  • Proof of accommodation in Taiwan

Fees and Processing Times

Fee Type Cost (NT$) Processing Time
Visa Application 3,600 7-10 working days
ARC Application 1,000 10-14 working days
Health Check 2,000 1-2 working days

Business Sectors

Priority Investment Areas:
  • Information Technology
  • Green Energy
  • Biotechnology
  • Smart Manufacturing
  • Digital Commerce
  • Financial Technology

Path to Permanent Residency

Requirements for APRC (Alien Permanent Resident Certificate):

  • 5 years of continuous residence
  • Maintaining investment requirements
  • No criminal record
  • Proof of financial stability
  • Basic Chinese language ability

FAQs

Yes, spouse and unmarried children under 20 can apply for dependent visas.

Yes, regular presence and active management of your investment is required to maintain visa status.
Quick Facts

  • Initial Visa: 1-3 years
  • Family Eligible: Yes
