Taiwan Investment Visa Guide 2024

Latest Update (March 2024): Taiwan has updated investment thresholds and introduced new incentives for foreign investors in strategic sectors.

What is the Taiwan Investment Visa?

The Taiwan Investment Visa is designed for foreign nationals who wish to invest significant capital in Taiwan's economy. This visa category allows investors to establish businesses, create employment opportunities, and contribute to Taiwan's economic growth while enjoying various benefits and privileges.

Investment Options

Available Investment Routes:
  • New Business Investment: NT$6 million minimum
  • Existing Business Investment: NT$8 million minimum
  • Government Bonds: NT$15 million minimum
  • Fixed Deposits: NT$15 million minimum
  • Real Estate Investment: NT$30 million minimum

Key Benefits

  • Long-term residency rights
  • Family inclusion benefits
  • Business ownership rights
  • Tax incentives for investments
  • Access to healthcare system
  • Path to permanent residency

Priority Business Sectors

Strategic Investment Areas:
  • Technology and Innovation
  • Green Energy
  • Smart Manufacturing
  • Digital Commerce
  • Biotechnology
  • Financial Services
  • Tourism and Hospitality
  • Infrastructure Development

Eligibility Requirements

Basic Requirements:
  • Meet minimum investment thresholds
  • Clean criminal record
  • Proof of investment funds source
  • Viable business plan
  • Management experience
  • Valid passport
Investment must be maintained throughout the visa period.

Application Process

  1. Business Registration

    Register company with Ministry of Economic Affairs

  2. Investment Verification

    Submit proof of investment and fund transfer

  3. Visa Application

    Apply at Taiwan overseas mission

  4. ARC Application

    Apply for Alien Resident Certificate

  5. Business Operation

    Begin business operations in Taiwan

Required Documents

  • Investment application forms
  • Business plan and feasibility study
  • Proof of investment funds
  • Bank statements
  • Company registration documents
  • Personal identification documents
  • Criminal record check
  • Professional certificates
  • Property documents (if applicable)
  • Tax clearance certificates

Fees and Processing Times

Fee Type Cost (NT$) Processing Time
Investment Application 5,000 4-6 weeks
Business Registration 15,000 2-3 weeks
Visa Processing 3,000 2-4 weeks

Tax Benefits and Incentives

Investment Advantages:
  • Tax holidays for new businesses
  • R&D tax credits
  • Import duty exemptions
  • Investment allowances
  • Accelerated depreciation
  • Export incentives

Permanent Residency Path

APRC Requirements:
  • Maintain investment for 5 years
  • Physical presence requirements
  • Business performance criteria
  • Employment creation targets
  • Clean record in Taiwan

FAQs

While most sectors are open for investment, some restricted industries require special approval. Priority sectors receive additional incentives.

As long as you maintain the required investment amount and comply with regulations, temporary business losses won't affect your visa status.
Quick Facts
  • Min Investment: NT$6M
  • Initial Stay: 3 years
  • Processing: 4-6 weeks
  • Family Eligible: Yes
