Dubai Tourist Visa Fees 2024

Quick Overview

Understanding Dubai tourist visa fees is essential for budget planning. This comprehensive guide provides detailed information about different visa types, processing fees, and additional costs.

Standard Visa Fees

Tourist Visas

Visa Type Duration Entry Type Processing Fee Total Cost (PKR)
30-Day Tourist Visa 30 days Single Entry PKR 15,000 PKR 25,000-35,000
30-Day Tourist Visa 30 days Multiple Entry PKR 20,000 PKR 30,000-40,000
60-Day Tourist Visa 60 days Single Entry PKR 25,000 PKR 35,000-45,000
90-Day Tourist Visa 90 days Single Entry PKR 35,000 PKR 45,000-55,000

Additional Costs

Processing Service Fees

  • Standard processing: Included in base fee
  • Express processing: +PKR 5,000
  • Premium processing: +PKR 10,000
  • Document attestation: PKR 2,000-5,000 per document

Insurance and Medical

  • Travel insurance: PKR 3,000-5,000
  • Medical screening (if required): PKR 5,000-8,000
  • Vaccination certificates: PKR 1,000-2,000

Cost Breakdown

30-Day Single Entry Tourist Visa

Base visa fee: PKR 15,000
Travel insurance: PKR 3,000
Document attestation: PKR 3,000
Service charges: PKR 4,000
Total estimated cost: PKR 25,000

Payment Methods

  • Bank transfer
  • Credit/Debit card
  • Cash payment at authorized centers
  • Online payment portals

Tips for Cost Optimization

  • Apply during off-peak seasons for better rates
  • Compare fees from multiple authorized agents
  • Book travel insurance in advance
  • Prepare documents properly to avoid additional attestation costs
  • Consider group applications for better rates
