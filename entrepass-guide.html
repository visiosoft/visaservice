Understanding the EntrePass

The EntrePass is designed for foreign entrepreneurs who want to start and operate a business in Singapore. This pass is particularly suited for innovative startups and businesses that contribute to Singapore's economic growth.

Eligibility Criteria

Entrepreneur Criteria:
  • Has funding from recognized VCs/investors
  • Holds intellectual property
  • Research collaboration with institutes
  • Strong business network
  • Track record of entrepreneurship
Company Requirements:
  • Registered company in Singapore
  • Minimum 30% shareholding
  • Paid-up capital of at least S$50,000
  • Business plan and projections
  • Innovative business model

Innovation Assessment
