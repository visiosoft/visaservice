Taiwan Special Professional Visa Guide 2024

Latest Update (March 2024): Taiwan has expanded the scope of Special Professional Visa categories and streamlined the application process.

What is the Special Professional Visa?

The Taiwan Special Professional Visa is designed for highly skilled foreign professionals in specific industries. This visa category offers enhanced benefits and a streamlined application process for qualified specialists.

Key Advantages
  • Fast-track processing
  • Higher salary threshold
  • Extended validity period (up to 3 years)
  • Easier path to permanent residence
  • Family benefits included

Eligible Industries and Professions

Technology & Innovation
  • AI/Machine Learning Specialists
  • Data Scientists
  • Cybersecurity Experts
  • Software Architects
Finance & Business
  • Investment Managers
  • Financial Analysts
  • Business Development Directors
  • Risk Management Specialists
Science & Research
  • Research Scientists
  • Biotechnology Specialists
  • Medical Researchers
  • Environmental Scientists
Education & Culture
  • University Professors
  • Cultural Exchange Experts
  • Language Education Specialists
  • Arts & Culture Professionals

Eligibility Requirements

Professional Requirements
  • Master's degree or higher in relevant field
  • Minimum 5 years of professional experience
  • Proven expertise in specialized field
  • Minimum salary threshold of NT$160,000/month
  • Recognition or awards in the field
Additional Criteria
  • Clean criminal record
  • Valid passport (at least 6 months)
  • Health check requirements
  • Professional certifications (if applicable)

Application Process

  1. Qualification Assessment

    Submit credentials for evaluation by relevant ministry.

  2. Document Preparation

    Gather and authenticate all required documents.

  3. Visa Application

    Apply through Taiwan overseas mission or online platform.

  4. Work Permit

    Apply for special professional work permit.

  5. ARC Application

    Apply for Alien Resident Certificate upon arrival.

Required Documents

Personal Documents
  • Passport copy
  • Professional certificates
  • Academic credentials
  • Criminal record check
  • Health certificate
Professional Documents
  • Employment contract
  • Professional achievements
  • Reference letters
  • Portfolio (if applicable)
  • Income proof

Benefits of Special Professional Visa

Professional Benefits
  • Fast-track processing
  • Multiple entry permit
  • Extended visa validity
  • Work permit flexibility
Family Benefits
  • Dependent visas for family
  • Spouse work rights
  • Children's education support
  • Healthcare coverage
