Saudi Arabia Work Visa Guide - Complete Guide 2024

Types of Saudi Arabia Work Visas

Saudi Arabia offers different types of work visas depending on your profession and employment type. Here's a comprehensive guide to help you understand the options:

1. Employment Visa (Work Visa)

For regular employment in Saudi Arabia
  • Requires job offer from Saudi employer
  • Valid for 1-2 years
  • Processing time: 2-4 weeks

2. Professional Work Visa

For highly skilled professionals
  • For doctors, engineers, and other professionals
  • Requires professional qualifications
  • Processing time: 3-4 weeks

3. Temporary Work Visa

For short-term employment
  • Valid for up to 6 months
  • For project-based work
  • Processing time: 2-3 weeks

Required Documents

  • Valid Passport (6 months validity)
  • Completed Visa Application Form
  • Passport-sized Photographs
  • Job Offer Letter
  • Educational Certificates
  • Professional Experience Certificates
  • Medical Certificate
  • Police Clearance Certificate

Application Process

  1. Receive job offer from Saudi employer
  2. Employer applies for work permit (Iqama)
  3. Gather required documents
  4. Complete visa application form
  5. Submit application at Saudi embassy/consulate
  6. Undergo medical examination
  7. Wait for visa processing
  8. Travel to Saudi Arabia
  9. Complete residence permit process

Processing Time and Fees

Visa Type Processing Time Fee (SAR)
Employment Visa 2-4 weeks 2000
Professional Work Visa 3-4 weeks 2500
Temporary Work Visa 2-3 weeks 1500

Tips for Successful Application

  • Ensure all documents are properly translated into Arabic
  • Verify the authenticity of your job offer
  • Complete medical examination at approved centers
  • Maintain clear criminal record
  • Follow Saudi Arabia's cultural and religious guidelines

Common Reasons for Rejection

  • Incomplete documentation
  • False information in application
  • Medical conditions
  • Criminal record
  • Previous visa violations
