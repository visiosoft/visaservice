Taiwan Alien Special Manpower Technical Permit (ASMTP) Guide 2024

Latest Update (March 2024): Taiwan has expanded the ASMTP program to include new technical fields and simplified the application process for specialized technical workers.

What is the Taiwan ASMTP?

The Alien Special Manpower Technical Permit (ASMTP) is a specialized work permit designed for foreign technical professionals with specific expertise needed in Taiwan's key industries. This permit allows skilled technical workers to contribute to Taiwan's industrial development while enjoying special benefits and streamlined processes.

Key Benefits

  • Fast-track processing
  • Multiple entry permit
  • Initial validity of up to 3 years
  • Family can join as dependents
  • Access to national health insurance
  • Tax benefits for technical expertise

Eligible Technical Fields

Priority Technical Areas:
  • Semiconductor Manufacturing
  • Advanced Manufacturing
  • Green Energy Technology
  • Biotechnology
  • Artificial Intelligence
  • Internet of Things (IoT)
  • Smart Machinery
  • Aerospace Engineering

Eligibility Requirements

Basic Requirements:
  • Technical expertise in eligible fields
  • Minimum 3 years relevant work experience
  • Bachelor's degree or higher in related field
  • Clean criminal record
  • Employer sponsorship
  • Meet minimum salary requirements
Applicants must demonstrate specialized technical skills not readily available in Taiwan.

Application Process

  1. Employer Application

    Taiwanese employer submits initial application

  2. Technical Assessment

    Evaluation of technical expertise and qualifications

  3. Work Permit Approval

    Issuance of ASMTP by labor authorities

  4. Visa Application

    Apply for resident visa at Taiwan embassy

  5. ARC Application

    Apply for ARC within 15 days of arrival

Required Documents

  • Completed application forms
  • Valid passport (at least 6 months validity)
  • Educational certificates
  • Technical certifications
  • Work experience letters
  • Employment contract
  • Company registration documents
  • Technical expertise proof
  • Police clearance certificate
  • Health examination report

Fees and Processing Times

Fee Type Cost (NT$) Processing Time
Work Permit 3,500 7-10 working days
Resident Visa 2,200 5-7 working days
ARC Application 1,000 10-14 working days

Salary and Benefits

Compensation Package:
  • Minimum monthly salary: NT$60,000
  • Technical expertise allowance
  • Health insurance coverage
  • Labor insurance benefits
  • Annual leave entitlement
  • Tax deductions for technical skills

Renewal and Long-term Options

Extension and Settlement:
  • Renewable every 3 years
  • APRC eligible after 5 years
  • Citizenship option available
  • Skills development programs
