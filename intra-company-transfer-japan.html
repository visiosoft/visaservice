Understanding the Intra-Company Transfer Visa

The Japan Intra-Company Transfer Visa (企業内転勤) is designed for employees of foreign companies who are being transferred to a branch office, subsidiary, or affiliate company in Japan. This visa category facilitates the international mobility of skilled professionals within multinational organizations.

Key Features:
  • Validity: 1-5 years (renewable)
  • Multiple entry permitted
  • Family can accompany
  • Work restricted to specific employer
  • Path to permanent residency available

Company Requirements

Japanese Office Requirements:
  • Registered business in Japan
  • Physical office location
  • Minimum operating history
  • Compliance with labor laws
  • Adequate business performance
Parent Company Requirements:
  • Direct ownership relationship
  • Active business operations
  • International presence
  • Financial stability
  • Transfer policy framework

Employee Eligibility

Basic Requirements:
Criteria Requirements

Required Documents

Personal Documents:
  • Valid passport
  • Professional certificates
  • Employment history
  • Educational documents
  • ID photographs
  • Resume in Japanese format
Company Documents:
  • Company registration (登記簿謄本)
  • Transfer order (辞令)
  • Business overview
  • Financial statements
  • Organization charts
  • Employment contract

Application Process

  1. Certificate of Eligibility (CoE)

    Japanese office applies for this on your behalf at the Immigration Bureau

  2. Document Submission

    Submit all required documents with translations

  3. CoE Issuance

    Processing time: 1-2 months

  4. Visa Application

    Apply at Japanese embassy/consulate in your country

  5. Entry to Japan

    Must enter within 3 months of visa issuance
