Understanding the Work Permit

The Work Permit is for semi-skilled foreign workers in the construction, manufacturing, marine shipyard, process or services sector. Different requirements and conditions apply based on your sector and country of origin.

Sector Categories

Available Sectors:
  • Construction Sector
  • Manufacturing Sector
  • Marine Shipyard Sector
  • Process Sector
  • Services Sector
  • Domestic Work Sector

Basic Requirements

General Criteria:
  • Age: 18 to 50 years (may vary by sector)
  • From an approved source country
  • Valid skills certification
  • Pass medical examination
  • Clean criminal record
Source Countries by Sector:
Sector Approved Countries
Construction Malaysia, China, India, Bangladesh, Thailand, Myanmar, Philippines
Manufacturing Malaysia, China, India, Bangladesh, Myanmar, Philippines
Marine Malaysia, China, India, Bangladesh, Myanmar, Philippines

Quota and Levy System

Dependency Ceiling Ratios:
Sector Maximum Ratio Basic Tier Levy Higher Tier Levy
Construction 1:7 $300 $600
Manufacturing 1:5 $250 $550
Marine 1:3 $300 $600

Required Documents

Personal Documents:
  • Valid passport
  • Skills qualification certificates
  • Medical examination results
  • Recent passport photos
  • Educational certificates
Employer Documents:
  • Business registration
  • Work contract
  • Security bond
  • Insurance policies
  • Quota eligibility proof

Application Process

  1. In-Principle Approval (IPA)

    Employer applies for IPA through WP Online

  2. Medical Examination

    Worker undergoes medical screening

  3. Security Bond

    Employer posts security bond

  4. Work Permit Card

    Collect card after approval

Employer Responsibilities

  • Provide acceptable accommodation
  • Purchase medical insurance
  • Pay monthly levy
  • Ensure safe working conditions
  • Maintain worker records
  • Repatriation responsibility

Common Issues & Solutions

  • Quota limits - Monitor workforce ratio
  • Skills verification - Ensure proper certification
  • Medical requirements - Regular check-ups
  • Accommodation standards - Meet MOM guidelines
  • Levy payment - Set up GIRO arrangement
Quick Facts
  • Processing: 1-3 weeks
  • Validity: Up to 2 years
  • Age Limit: 18-50 years
  • Quota: Sector-based
  • Levy: From $250
