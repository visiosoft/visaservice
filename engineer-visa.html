Understanding the Engineer Visa

The Engineer Visa is designed for technical professionals who want to work in Japan's thriving technology sector. This visa category covers various technical fields including IT, software development, electronics, mechanical engineering, and more.

Eligibility Requirements

Educational Requirements:
  • Bachelor's degree in relevant field
  • Technical certification equivalents
  • 10+ years experience (in lieu of degree)
Professional Requirements:
  • Job offer from Japanese company
  • Minimum salary requirements
  • Relevant technical skills
  • Basic Japanese language ability
Technical Fields Covered:
  • Software Development
  • System Architecture
  • Network Engineering
  • Database Administration
  • AI/Machine Learning
  • Hardware Engineering

Application Process

  1. Certificate of Eligibility

    Your employer applies for this document

  2. Document Preparation

    Gather and authenticate required documents

  3. Visa Application

    Submit application at Japanese embassy

  4. Visa Collection

    Collect visa and prepare for relocation

Required Documents

Personal Documents:
  • Valid passport
  • Degree certificates
  • Technical certifications
  • Resume/CV
  • Passport photos
Professional Documents:
  • Employment contract
  • Company registration
  • Job description
  • Salary details
  • Technical project portfolio

Salary Requirements

Experience Level Minimum Annual Salary (JPY) Benefits Required
Entry Level (0-3 years) 3,000,000 Health Insurance, Pension
Mid Level (3-5 years) 4,000,000 Health Insurance, Pension, Housing
Senior Level (5+ years) 5,000,000+ Comprehensive Package

Visa Duration and Renewal

  • Initial duration: 1-5 years
  • Renewable based on employment
  • Path to permanent residency
  • Family visa options available

Common Challenges & Solutions

  • Language barrier - Take Japanese classes
  • Technical assessment - Prepare portfolio
  • Salary requirements - Negotiate package
  • Document authentication - Use certified services
  • Cultural adaptation - Join expat networks
Quick Facts
  • Processing: 1-3 months
  • Validity: 1-5 years
  • Minimum Salary: ¥3M/year
  • Education: Bachelor's
  • Japanese: Basic level
