UK Skilled Worker Visa Requirements - Complete Guide 2024

Advertisement

Key Points

  • Valid job offer from a UK employer with a sponsor license
  • Job must be on the eligible occupations list
  • Minimum salary requirement: £26,200 per year
  • English language proficiency required
  • Processing time: 3-8 weeks

Eligibility Requirements

Basic Requirements

  • Valid Certificate of Sponsorship (CoS)
  • Job offer from an approved UK employer
  • Required skill level (RQF 3 or above)
  • English language proficiency (B1 level)
  • Minimum salary requirements met

Required Documents

  • Valid passport
  • Certificate of Sponsorship reference number
  • Proof of English language ability
  • Bank statements showing maintenance funds
  • TB test results (if applicable)
  • Criminal record certificate (if required)

Salary Requirements

Experience Level Minimum Salary Going Rate
New Entrant £20,960 70% of going rate
Experienced Worker £26,200 100% of going rate
Shortage Occupation £20,960 80% of going rate

Application Process

  1. Obtain a valid job offer from a UK employer
  2. Receive Certificate of Sponsorship from employer
  3. Prepare all required documents
  4. Complete online application form
  5. Pay application fee and healthcare surcharge
  6. Book biometric appointment
  7. Submit application and wait for decision

Processing Time and Fees

Processing Times

  • Standard: 8 weeks
  • Priority: 5 working days
  • Super Priority: 24 hours

Application Fees

  • Standard: £719
  • Priority: £1,500
  • Super Priority: £1,000
  • Healthcare Surcharge: £624 per year

Common Questions

Yes, you can switch to a Skilled Worker Visa from most other UK visas while in the UK, except for visitor visas and short-term study visas.

The general minimum salary requirement is £26,200 per year, but this can vary based on your occupation and experience level.

Yes, you can bring your spouse/partner and children under 18. They will need to apply for dependent visas and meet certain requirements.
